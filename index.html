Astarag means Asta (sunset) and rag (red colour) in Odia, my native language. I am passionate about building responsible machine learning systems that support thoughtful decision-making across industries.

Thank you for stopping by! I am currently working as an ML Engineer at Evidium while completing my master's in Data Science at Indiana University. I focus on building practical, responsible ML systems that bridge research and impact.

My curiosity for AI began in 2018 after watching DeepMind's AlphaGo defeat Lee Sedol with its legendary move 37. Since then, I have been building projects that explore how humans and intelligent machines can collaborate. When I am not working on ML systems, you will find me writing about ideas that fascinate me—ranging from AI and technology to economics and curious conspiracy theories—or playing John Mayer songs on my guitar.

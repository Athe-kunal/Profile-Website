Astarag means Asta (sunset) and rag (red colour) in Odia, my native language.

My curiosity for AI began in 2018 after watching DeepMind's AlphaGo defeat Lee Sedol with its legendary move 37. It was crazy for me when Lee Sedol went for a cigarette break to handle the pressure. Since then, I have been building projects that explore how humans and intelligent machines can collaborate. When I am not working on ML systems, you will find me writing about ideas or playing John Mayer songs on my guitar. Read more on my blog.
